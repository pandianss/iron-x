# STANDARD: Professional Ethics of Execution (ODS-ETH-001)

## 1. The Obligation to Execute
Professionals (Engineers, Managers, Doctors, Administrators) have an ethical obligation not just to *intend* good outcomes, but to establish the *conditions* that ensure them.

## 2. Systemic Failure Modes
It is considered a **failure of professional system design** to:
1.  Accept a critical responsibility without a corresponding systemic support mechanism.
2.  Design a system that relies solely on human vigilance for safety-critical steps.
3.  Remove or disable reliability tools without a formal risk assessment.

## 3. The "Resilient Support" Principle
A professional's system must continue to function even when the individual is absent or distracted.
*   **Standard:** If your system relies on heroics to avoid collapse, the system design requires refinement.
*   **Duty:** You must build environments that protect the mission from human fallibility.

## 4. Institutional Norms
Organizations adopting this code should:
*   Reward **System Builders** who create sustainable reliability.
*   Treat **Performance Data** as a tool for collective growth, not punitive monitoring.

## 5. Reliability Commitments (Draft)
"I will prioritize systems over heroics. I will support my future self and my team through resilient environments. I will design for human success, acknowledging human fallibility."
